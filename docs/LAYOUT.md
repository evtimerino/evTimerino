## Buttons

|Icon|Function|Longpress function|

|![expose](/images/expose.png)|Start or pause the timer||
|![focus](/images/focus.png)| Switch on/off the lamp | Switch off oled display |
|![up](/images/up.png)|Increase the exposure time||
|![down](/images/down.png)| Decrease the exposure time||
